Paws Academy 

At Pampered Pets, We believe in teaching
your dog with kindness and care not through
fear or compulsion. We understand the impo-
rtance of learning. We only use reward based
training. This allows your dog to be happy even
while training.

We aim to make learning fun and enjoyable
for both owner and pet. Our trainers are
highly qualified in many different sections
and strive to take the pressure of training
of owners.

Behaviour Consultation

At Pampered Pets, we are dedicated to helping pets
and their families to create a connection and
relationship by helping pets We specialise in helping
pets in need that have a hard time gaining trust or had
a traumatic thing happen in their life. We prioritise in
making your pet feel safe and working together one
-to-one with the owner to make that trust flow.
