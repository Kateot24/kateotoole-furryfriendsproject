Concerned about where your pet will stay?

At The Pawsome Inn 

We provide a state of the art Hotel Inn
for furry friend parents who are looking for
a trip away and need a welcoming place to
keep their furry friends safe. Your wonderful
furry friends stay in our Daycare centre during
the day, When night-time falls they have a
fabulous stay in our on-site Inn. Pets will be
seperated during their nights stay unless.
customers request otherwise.

Our Inn Facilities

- State of the art, custom built suite,

- Each suite is provided with CCTV cameras for safety.

- Human-size toddler beds for our purfect pets comfort
with heated private rooms.

- Each pet will be handled by one of our staff
with cuddles and affection to relax and comfort your
friend before bed.

- In any emergencies, we have a doctor on board
especially for any pets that need any special needs.

Our Petite Suites

For our smaller guests,we have a single & two dorm
style if your pet prefers to have company overnight. With
parents with more than one pet, we also have group cubicles

Please remember we allow overnight stays to pets who
have passed our daycare assessment or are regular
grooming clients. To book, please contact us
