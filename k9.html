Canine Training

At Paw's Academy we specialise in professional sport and utility.
training with the use of positive reinforcement, Our aim is to
reinforce the bond between dogs and their owners to create
a habit of obedience. Our trainings consist in changing the
behaviour of a dog by increasing obedience and discipline
which allows dog to find their real place with their born nature,
aswell as creating a truly friendly bond between dog and owner

What we teach

- Proper interaction between both dogs and people

- Walking on a leash next to owner/ offleash

- Fetching

- Staying in place without the owner

- Elimination of negative behaviour (jumping on people,barking at other dogs, etc.)
